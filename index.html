Health

Viruses

Ferrets are susceptible to colds and flus. Symptoms are similar to human illness, and can be treated with an antihistamine.

Diet

A good diet consists of high protein ferret chow fed two to four times a day. In addition they can be given meat based treats, cooked eggs, and cooked meat.

Vitamins

For optimal health, ferrets require vitamins and supplements just like people do. Such supplements include digestive, fur, and high calorie treatments.

Heat Stroke

Ferrets cannot sweat, making it hard for them to tolerate any temperature above 80 degrees. A heavily panting ferret may indicate over heating, and should be covered in cool towels.
